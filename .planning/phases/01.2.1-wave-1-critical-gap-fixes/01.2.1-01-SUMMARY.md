## Decisions Made

- **buildAclPath signature:** Takes pre-assembled stanzaKey rather than reconstructing from realm+name — the caller knows the stanza format. This matches legacy `password-crud.js` line 456 pattern where `stanza` already includes trailing colon.
- **createCredential stanzaKey source:** Uses `created.entry[0].name` from Splunk response instead of editLink — editLink-based ACL paths were a workaround that could produce incorrect paths with double-encoded segments.
